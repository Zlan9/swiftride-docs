# 📊 SwiftRide Project Summary

## 📌 Overview
SwiftRide is a modern ride-sharing application designed to connect passengers with drivers in real-time. It focuses on providing a seamless, safe, and cost-effective transportation experience.

## 🎯 Purpose
The main purpose of SwiftRide is to simplify urban commuting by offering:
- Easy ride booking
- Real-time driver tracking
- Transparent pricing

## 👥 Target Users
- Daily commuters
- Students and working professionals
- Tourists and occasional travelers
- Drivers looking for flexible earning opportunities

## ✨ Key Features
- 📍 Real-time GPS tracking
- 💳 Multiple payment options (cash, card, wallet)
- ⭐ Driver and rider rating system
- 🔒 Safety features (ride tracking & SOS)
- 📱 User-friendly mobile interface

## 🚀 Vision
To become a reliable and widely used ride-sharing platform that improves urban mobility and reduces travel inefficiencies.
